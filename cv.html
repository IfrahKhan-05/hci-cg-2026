Education & Background

  • Studied Psychology and Cognitive Science – foundation for understanding user behavior
  • Engineering knowledge helped in design of interactive systems

Major Contributions

User-Centered Design

Introduced principles of designing systems around user goals, tasks, and mental models.

Seven Stages of Action

Outlined how users form goals, execute actions, perceive outcomes, and evaluate results.

Bridging Gulfs

Conceptualized the Gulf of Execution and Gulf of Evaluation to identify interaction challenges.

Impact on HCI

Norman’s frameworks directly influenced usability evaluation methods, including how designers apply GOMS and KLM models to predict user performance and improve interface design.
